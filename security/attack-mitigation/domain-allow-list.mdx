Domain allow-listing is a security feature in BESPIN Airmen Data Platform (powered by Rownd) that provides an additional layer of protection for your application. By specifying a list of approved domains, you can ensure that BESPIN Airmen Data Platform (powered by Rownd) only authenticates users from the designated websites. This helps prevent unauthorized access attempts and keeps your user data secure. By using domain allow-listing in BESPIN Airmen Data Platform (powered by Rownd), you can take greater control over your app's security, giving you and your users peace of mind.

## Configuring the domain allow-list

1. Open your app in the [BESPIN Airmen Data Platform (powered by Rownd) platform](https://https://app.dev.rownd.teambespin.us/applications). Select **Settings** from the left sidebar.

  ![Finding Settings is easy; just select "Settings" from the HOME screen.](/images/settings-page.png)

2. Locate the **URL(s) you are installing BESPIN Airmen Data Platform (powered by Rownd) on** section
3. Enter the domains where you are installing BESPIN Airmen Data Platform (powered by Rownd) with a "," between each. Please include any dev, test, and prod domains where BESPIN Airmen Data Platform (powered by Rownd) may be installed.

  <Info>
    Be sure to include all subdomians including `www` if applicable.
  </Info>

<Note>
  Althogh BESPIN Airmen Data Platform (powered by Rownd) may load on non-allow-listed sites, users will not be able to authenticate.
</Note>

## Finishing up

Once you have completed adding domains, press **Save Edits** to ensure your domain list is saved.
